Digital transformation consulting services

We help local businesses capitalize on the opportunities presented by change through the intelligent use of technology.

We are curious, open-minded and action-oriented people who enjoy solving complex problems. Our team is composed of business people with a wealth of experience in technology, sales, management and communications.
Over the past 20 years, we have built a solid network of partners in a wide range of industries. We use our expertise and resources to assist managers who want to quickly generate tangible results.

Services

We advise our clients on the best ways to use technology as a competitive advantage. We help businesses select, configure and effectively use the right technological tools. This also includes finding technology partners and recruiting and training human resources.
Our contribution addresses different aspects of the digital transformation:
Business and technology strategy
E-commerce optimization
Technology audit
Technology platform selection
Technical team evaluation and training
Coaching Agile
Supplier evaluation and selection
Executive coaching
